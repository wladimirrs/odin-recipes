Garlic Kale

How to make easy Garlic Kale

It is a 3 ingredients dish that can be done within 15 minutes.

  • Kale
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  1. Soak kale leaves in a large bowl of water until dirt and sand begin to fall to the bottom.
  2. Chop the kale leaves into 1-inch pieces.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until sizzling, about 1 minute.
  4. Add kale to the skillet and place a cover over the top.
  5. Cook, stirring occasionally with tongs, until kale is bright green and slightly tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Enjoy!
